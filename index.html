Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

A healthy individual may donate every three months.

Does donating blood hurt?

Only a quick pinch when the needle is inserted; most donors feel fine.

Is donating blood safe?

Yes, sterile needles and equipment are used only once.

What should I do before donating?

Eat a healthy meal, drink plenty of water, and get enough rest.

Can I donate with a tattoo or piercing?

If the tattooing procedure or the piercing was done a year ago, he/she may donate. This is also applicable to acupuncture, and other procedures involving needles.

Will donating blood make me weak?

No, it will not make you weak. Donating 450cc will not cause any ill effects or weakness. The human body has the capacity to compensate for the new fluid volume.
